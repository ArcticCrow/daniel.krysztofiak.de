TOWER OF BABEL
WIP 3D STANDALONE ROGUELIKE RPG WITH UNITY AND PROCEDURAL CONTENT GENERATION

Tower of Babel that I thought of a couple of years ago, but had to put aside for some time due to school and exams. It's meant to be a roguelike game with procedural dungeons spread out over multiple "towers". The most interesting part about it, was figuring out how to generate those towers entirely with code down to their smallest details and designing/researching algorithms that would help me achieve this. It's currently not being worked on, but will be picked up again soon, and this time with better insight due to a couple of finished projects.
