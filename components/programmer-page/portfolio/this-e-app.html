THIS E! APP
ANDROID APPLICATION WITH JAVA

ThisE!app was an android application that two friends and I made for a Streamer who plays games and creates remixes as a DJ on twitch.tv and is called thisE!guy. We were given a few given design guidelines and created an application which featured his Twitter feed, a special in-app feed, a listing of his past streams and stream highlights, a live and set release notification and a media player to stream or download and listen to his sets. We created this app over the course of 6 months in our free time next to preparations for our final exams.
